Grading Policy

For any answer, grading is based on correctness, clarity, and observation of instructions.

  • Midterm: 20 - 25%
  • Final Exam: 20 - 25%
  • Assignments: 35%
  • Projects: 20%

Between the two exams, the higher score will count as 25% and the lower as 20% towards your final grade.

Detailed Final Grade Calculation

Let:

  • Midterm total score = y1    Your Midterm score = x1
  • Final Exam total score = y2    Your Final Exam score = x2
  • HW (Assignments) total score = y3    Your Total HW score = x3
  • Project total score = y4    Your Project score = x4
The weighted formula for your final grade is: 

Your Total of Final Grade = (x1 / y1) * 200 + (x2 / y2) * 250 + (x3 / y3) * 350 + (x4 / y4) * 200

Letter grades are assigned as follows:

  • A ∈ [900, 1000]
  • B ∈ [800, 900)
  • C ∈ [700, 800)
  • D ∈ [600, 700)
  • F ∈ [0, 600)

Note: The Final Exam will be a take-home exam.
